Aperture control mechanism

The IVC Plasmainstrument company manufactured and shipped a former-vulcanizer diaphragm control mechanism for one of the leaders in Russian tire production. The mechanism was equipped with high-temperature seals from Trelleborg, hydraulic and pneumatic drives, and high-precision position sensors. In the manufacturing process, technological processes of high-frequency processing, chrome plating and grinding, precision turning, deep drilling, as well as other processes were used.
