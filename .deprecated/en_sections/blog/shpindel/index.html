Pneumatic spindle

The IVC Plasmainstrument company manufactured and supplied a pneumatic spindle for the tire molding machine TR72 (analogous to Pirelli) to the flagship of Russian tire production. As part of the product manufacturing project, the IVC Plasmainstrument company revised the design documentation, drew the project in 3D graphics, identified bottlenecks and agreed on changes to the design documentation. Also, during the manufacturing process of the product, seven new technological operations were applied, including deep boring and grinding, cutting long internal grooves, and deep drilling. Equipment for product testing has been manufactured. The supplied products meet all stated requirements. Thank you for your trust.
