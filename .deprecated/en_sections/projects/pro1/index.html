Application of decorative coatings to ceramic tiles

Application of decorative coatings on ceramic tiles in “gold”, “silver”, “anthracite”, “bronze” tones.
Our company's specialists have developed, manufactured and tested a magnetron with a heated target. Tests showed good results for applying ceramic coatings: oxides and nitrides. 
A prototype was manufactured, technical and economic parameters were obtained, and a patent application was filed.
Project stage: proven scalable technology introduced into production.
