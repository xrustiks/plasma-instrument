Modernization of vacuum installations

Every year, consumers of vacuum coating systems have increasingly high demands on coatings and equipment performance. On the other hand, there are no financial resources to purchase expensive equipment. At the same time, there is an oversupply of installations on the market that are suitable for upgrading equipment, including at the customer’s. In this case, the best option is to modernize vacuum equipment, increasing the productivity or functionality of the equipment.

IVC Plasmainstrument has extensive experience and proven solutions in the field of equipment modernization.

Advantages of working with us:

  • Ready-made, proven solutions allow work to be completed in the shortest possible time.
  • there is extensive experience in servicing ion plasma sources and vacuum equipment, replacing electric drives of valves with a pneumatic drive, which saves resources and time;
  • cooperation with leading dealers of vacuum equipment guarantees receiving the optimal offer for components;
  • fast design and execution time;
  • Our employees carry out all types of work: installation, dismantling of installations, machining of parts and production of equipment, vacuum-tight welding and leak testing with a helium leak detector, vacuum-tight silver soldering, setting up automation and writing and adjusting installation control programs.
