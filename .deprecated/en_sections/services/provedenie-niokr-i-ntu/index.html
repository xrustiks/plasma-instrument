High-tech machining using thermal and galvanic processes

The IVC Plasmainstrument company will manufacture high-tech products according to customer drawings using machining centers, turning, boring, milling, electrical discharge cutting, heat treatment, galvanic work, and hardening processes in a vacuum.
 
Among the proven technologies:
  • Polished chrome-plated, hardened sleeves and rods, with a diameter of up to 200 mm, a length of up to 2400 mm, with a runout of 0.2 mm/1 m, with a hardness after high-frequency testing of 60HRC,
  • Manufacturing of heavily loaded conveyor rails from defect-free hardening of the surface of steel 65G (and other steels) to hardness 60HRC with surface roughness Ra1.6
  • Manufacturing of non-standard subs according to customer drawings
  • Production of spare parts for import substitution for technological equipment, including equipment not serviced by the manufacturer.
  • Manufacturing of welded products, followed by five-axis processing of product dimensions XYZ 5300mmx2200mmx800mm, weighing up to 30 tons, including those made of heat-resistant alloys;
  • Urgent production of parts for emergency equipment
When developing a project, we carefully study, together with the customer, the operating conditions of the product, the material being processed, the service life of the product, and the quality of the surface. If it is difficult to obtain the required characteristics for the manufacture of products in Russia, we place orders with partners in South Korea, Germany, Finland, and Turkey.
To solve problems related to the scientific component of the project, we cooperate with the country's leading scientific centers: MISiS, Moscow Higher Technical University named after. Bauman, KFU.

The experience and professionalism of our employees allows us to solve problems that other companies do not undertake.
