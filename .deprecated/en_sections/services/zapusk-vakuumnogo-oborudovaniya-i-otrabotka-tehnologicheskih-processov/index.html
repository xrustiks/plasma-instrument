Launch of vacuum equipment and testing of technological processes

Our company’s specialists have accumulated extensive experience in launching vacuum equipment for coating and developing technological processes for coating with ion-plasma sources:

  • Metal: Ti, Cr, Zr, Al and other metals. Deposition of magnetic materials using a magnetron with a heated target: Ni, Co, stainless steel. These coatings are used as adhesive and functional layers in optics, microelectronics, when applying hardening coatings and other areas. (Metalization of plastic, furniture and door fittings),
  • Semiconductor: Si, Ge, ZnO, ZnS, SiC and other coatings used as functional layers of microelectronics,
  • Ceramic: TiN, CrN AlTiN, TixOy, AlTiSiN, TiCN, CrxOy, Al2O3 and many others, used as strengthening, protective and decorative, photocatalytic coatings. (tools, stamps, molds, furniture and door fittings, decorative ceramics)
