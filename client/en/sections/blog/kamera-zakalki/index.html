Quenching chamber

The IVC Plasmainstrument company has modernized the hardening chambers of tool steels for one of the leaders in the production of specialized tools for the health and beauty industry. In the process of modernizing the chamber, thermal and strength calculations, metallurgical analysis of materials and welding technologies used were carried out.
The hardness of steel 40X13 after vacuum bright hardening was 52 HRC. Thermal calculations carried out showed the potential for increasing hardness up to 60HRC and reducing the hardening cycle time.
Modernization of vacuum hardening chambers has reduced the number of defects, time for changing equipment and equipment downtime.
Enterprises that have incandescent furnaces can expand the list of thermal operations performed (vacuum light hardening, vacuum light annealing, and others) when using vacuum hardening chambers. Calculations have shown that the use of traditional incandescent furnaces with a vacuum hardening chamber is 20 times cheaper than traditional vacuum furnaces. We invite all interested companies to cooperate in the field of application of vacuum hardening chambers.
