Reactor

The IVC Plasmainstrument company manufactured and shipped a reactor made of stainless steel and intended for scientific research. This reactor is equipped with a heater, temperature sensor and heat exchanger. The peculiarity of the manufacture of small reactors is that they do not fall under the requirements of Rostechnadzor, which allows our company to maintain reasonable prices for the manufacture of reactors.
