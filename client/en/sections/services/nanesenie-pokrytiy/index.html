Design and production of molds and dies

We manufacture structures of the following types of molding equipment:

  • Molds for thermoplastic casting;
  • Molds for pressing thermosets;
  • Molds for pressing rubber mixtures;
  • Molds for casting aluminum alloys;
  • Lost wax casting molds;
  • Molds for casting compounds and resins;
  • Chills for casting non-ferrous metals;
  • Dies;
When developing a project, we carefully study, together with the customer, the operating conditions of the equipment, the material being processed, the service life of the products, and the quality of the surface. Technological equipment is manufactured according to the product model. The CAD software used takes into account the thermal expansion of the tooling and product during operation and adjusts the dimensions of the tooling to obtain accurate dimensions.  After the tooling has been manufactured, trial tests are carried out at the factory to measure the resulting geometry of the part. If necessary, the equipment is adjusted with repeated testing.
Our company continuously monitors new trends in the field of materials science, new methods of mechanical processing of metal and heat treatment of metals, as well as the application of functional vacuum coatings using PVD technologies (wear-resistant and anti-adhesive properties). The application of chromium nitride (Cr) coatings has been developed and introduced into productionxNat), using the HIPIMS method, which allows you to increase the service life of the equipment several times.

All of the above allows us to perform the most complex tasks in the manufacture of technological equipment in the shortest possible time.
