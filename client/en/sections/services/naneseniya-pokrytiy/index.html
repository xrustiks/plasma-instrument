Coatings

The company offers vacuum metallization services (coating with magnetron and arc sources), which allow you to expand the range of your products and explore the possibilities of new solutions without extra costs for equipment and personnel.

Among the services provided:

  • Application of protective and decorative coatings in tones: chrome, silver, gold (titanium nitride)
  • Application of coatings using varnish-metal-varnish technology;
  • Application of wear-resistant, hardening coatings;

Product materials can include parts made of metals, plastics (ABS, polyamide, polycarbonate, etc.), glass and ceramics.

Functional purpose of coatings:

  • decorative;
  • wear-resistant;
  • antistatic;
  • functional;

Among the products we have coated: furniture fittings, plumbing components, heated towel rails, interior and exterior parts of cars and motorcycles, instrument housings, cutting tools.

The main costs when organizing the production of coatings in a vacuum fall on technological installations and equipment. The services provided by our company allow you to avoid the cost of purchasing it and pay only operating costs. Issues with the personnel who must service the installations are also eliminated.

Our company is ready to apply a free trial spraying (offer for serial product manufacturers).

How this happens in practice:

If there is a need for coating, the customer, having previously contacted us, sends a pilot batch of product samples with a description of the type of coating and its purpose. If the customer does not know the required coating of the product, but has a sample, the sample is sent for analysis. After this, we apply the required coating and send it to the customer. If the coating suits the customer, we develop equipment to launch the project into series. We agree with the customer on the schedule for the supply of blanks and shipment of coated products.

When expanding the volume of spraying, when it is rational to purchase your own equipment, we are ready to supply a vacuum installation with related equipment (washing cabinets, drying cabinets, polishing machines, etc.) At the same time, we will tell you where to purchase consumables, components and spare parts. We will help with calculating the cost of products, the cost of processes: washing, polishing, drying, coating. We will train staff in cases where difficulties arise in the technological process. We are always ready to provide assistance and advice.

Thus, you receive a ready-made solution that allows you, without interruptions in work and with minimal risks, to scale up the application of coatings from single batches to mass production.
